Sunday Roast

Village Hangout Cafe organized its first Sunday roast event on 7th May. A Sunday roast is a traditional British gathering where friends and family come together to enjoy a hearty meal typically served on Sundays. The centerpiece of the meal is a roasted joint of meat, such as beef or chicken, accompanied by roasted potatoes, Yorkshire pudding, vegetables like carrots and peas, and rich gravy. The roast is cooked slowly, allowing the meat to become tender and flavorful, while the potatoes and vegetables roast in the same oven. It's a social occasion that brings people together, offering a comforting and satisfying dining experience.
